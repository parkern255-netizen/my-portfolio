About

I'm Nathan Parker — a videographer, photographer, and digital storyteller based in Kennesaw, GA. My work lives at the intersection of visual craft and community: I want to find the images and sequences that make people feel something about the place they live in.

Background in live radio broadcast, short-form video production, social media strategy, and published sports journalism. Currently pursuing a B.S. in Digital Media & Communications at Georgia Highlands College (Honors College).
